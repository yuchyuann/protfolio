GOOD BOX

This summer, I attended TISP2019 program at the University of Tokyo and came up with this idea. It called Goodbox and cooperated with a local company 'Goodwork' in Kagawa, Japan. The main idea is changing the function of the original packages into delivering coded messages. Each pattern stands for a different character, and it can be combined into a short sentence.
