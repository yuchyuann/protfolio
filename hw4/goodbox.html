GOOD BOX

This summer, I attended TISP2019 program in University of Tokyo and came up with this idea. It called Goodbox and cooperated with local company in Kagawa, Japan. The main idea is changing the function of the original packages into dilivering coded messages. Each pattern stands for different character, and it can be combined into a short sentence.
